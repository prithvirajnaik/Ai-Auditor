## FAQ

**Is this free?**  
Yes. The audit and shareable report are free. We capture your email so we can send you the report and follow up with optimization tips — that's it.

**How accurate are the savings estimates?**  
The calculations are based on verified pricing from vendor websites (last checked May 2026). Seat minimum rules (e.g., Claude Team billing 5 seats minimum) are documented from official pricing pages. That said, pricing changes — we recommend verifying the specific figures before making cancellation decisions.

**Does Auto Audit access my billing accounts?**  
No. You enter your subscription data manually. We don't connect to Stripe, credit cards, or any billing provider. This keeps setup fast and requires no OAuth permissions.

**What happens to my data?**  
Audit results are stored with a random ID. When you share a public report URL, the company name, domain, and department names are anonymized — viewers see "Anonymous Startup" and "Team A" instead of your actual details.

**What AI tools does it support?**  
Currently: Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, v0, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API. More tools can be added on request.

**Can I run an audit without creating an account?**  
Yes. You can complete a full audit and view the results without signing up. Create an account to save your audit history and access the analytics dashboard.
